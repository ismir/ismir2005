Monday 12 September 2005: Concert
Venue: The Spitz, 109 Commercial Street, Old Spitalfields Market, E1 6BG

From Queen Mary, walk to Stepney Green Underground Station. Take the District or Hammersmith & City Line to Aldgate East. Walk up Commercial Street for about 10 mins. The Spitz is on the left.
Time: about 30 mins.

Further information:

  • The Spitz: Location and Access
  • Map from Streetmap.co.uk

Tuesday 13 September 2005: Reception
Venue: British Library, St Pancras, 96 Euston Road, London, NW1 2DB

From Queen Mary, walk to Stepney Green Underground Station. Take the Hammersmith & City Line to King's Cross St Pancras (if no Hammersmith & City Line train is shown on the display board, take the first District Line train and change at Aldgate East). Exit King's Cross St Pancras towards Euston Road (North Side). Turn right and walk along Euston Road, past the major building works at St Pancras Station, to reach the British Library.
Time: about 40 mins.

Further information:

  • British Library: Locations
  • British Library: Orientation and Map

Wednesday 14 September 2005: Banquet
Venue: Bank Westminster, 45 Buckingham Gate, London SW1E 6BS

From Queen Mary, walk to Stepney Green Underground Station. Take the District Line to St James's Park. Take the Petty France exit, turn left and walk until you reach Buckingham Gate. Turn left again and Bank Westminster is one minute walk down Buckingham Gate on your right.
Time: about 40 mins.
