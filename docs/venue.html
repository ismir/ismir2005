Venue

The ISMIR 2005 conference (Mon 12 - Thu 15 Sept 2005) will be held at the Mile End Campus of Queen Mary, University of London.

The ISMIR 2005 tutorials (Sun 11 Sept 2005) will be held at Goldsmiths College, University of London.

Conference venue (Queen Mary, Mile End Campus)

Queen Mary is a multi-faculty university about 15 minutes east of Central London. It is on a campus site bordered by Regents Canal and close to Docklands.

The venue is easily accessible by public transport. It is within a five minute walk of both Mile End Underground station (Central, District, and Hammersmith & City lines) and Stepney Green Underground station (District, and Hammersmith & City lines).
