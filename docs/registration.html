Registration Desk

For delegates arriving on Sunday 11 September, a Registration Desk will be open 18:00 - 21:00 Sunday 11 September in the Arts Building, Queen Mary, Mile End Campus. On Monday 12 September to Thursday 15 September, the Registration Desk will be in the People's Palace Foyer.

For Queen Mary campus and building locations, see: Queen Mary, Mile End Campus.

Note: We encourage delegates to register on Sunday, to avoid the Monday morning rush!

If attending Tutorials, please go directly to the Tutorial Venue, Goldsmiths College.

For directions on how to reach Goldsmiths College or Queen Mary, Mile End Campus, see: Arrival.

Registration Fees

NB: For each accepted paper/poster/demo, at least one author has to register for the conference (see Detailed Call for Submissions).

Conference registration includes all ISMIR 2005 conference sessions, lunches and refreshments. The registration fee also includes:

  • British Library Reception (R)
  • Concert (C)
  • Conference Banquet (B) [Standard & Full Student only]
  • Printed Proceedings (P) [Standard & Full Student only]
